About Biography Georgy L. Kapchits was born on October 2, 1939. In 1967, he graduated from the Institute of Asian and African Studies at Moscow State University, majoring in Somali and Amharic languages. From 1968, he worked at the Institute of Linguistic Studies of the Russian Academy of Sciences, defending his Candidate dissertation in 1975 and his Doctorate in Philology in 1990. His main research areas include Somali syntax, temporal lexicon, kinship terminology, and the collection of Somali proverbs and folklore. He participated in more than ten international Africanist conferences and led multiple grant projects in Russia and abroad.
