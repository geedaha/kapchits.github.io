Projects

Projects & Grants Dr. Kapchits has led multiple research projects funded by Russian and international agencies:

“Somali Language Documentation” (Grant № RU-2010-47, 2010–2012) Funding: Russian Science Foundation Goal: Create annotated corpus of Somali oral narratives. Outcome: Digital archive of 200+ transcripts, available upon request. “Kinship and Social Structure in Somali Clans” (Grant № RU-2015-21, 2015–2018) Funding: Russian Humanitarian Science Foundation Goal: Document kinship terms and marriage customs in Hargeisa region. Outcome: Monograph (2018), database of kinship terms (150+ entries).
