Supervisor Contact
To apply for academic supervision with Dr. Georgy Kapchits, please follow these steps:

Prepare a brief research proposal (1–2 pages) outlining your topic and methodology.
Email your CV and proposal to kapchits@example.com with the subject “Supervision Request.”
Briefly describe your academic background, previous projects, and publication list (if any).
Allow up to two weeks for a response; Dr. Kapchits conducts reviews every second Tuesday of the month.

Office location:
Moscow, Leninsky Prospect 32, Building 3, Institute of Linguistics, Room 415.
Office hours: Wednesdays 14:00–16:00 (MSK) or by appointment.
