Kinship Terms
Below is a selection of Somali and Oromo kinship terminology documented by Dr. Kapchits:

Somali TermEnglish EquivalentNotes
aabafatherAlso used honorifically as uncle.
hooyomotherOften appears in compound kin terms.
walaalsiblingGender-neutral term: brother/sister.
abtimaternal uncleLiterally “mother’s brother.”
