Course Syllabus
“Introduction to Somali Language and Culture”
Course Description:
An overview of Somali phonology, morphology, syntax, and sociolinguistic context. Includes readings in both Somali and English.
Weekly Schedule:

Week 1: Alphabet, orthography, and phonetics.
Week 2: Nominal morphology (gender, number, case).
Week 3: Verb morphology and tense-aspect-mood.
…
Week 12: Oral tradition and proverbs study.

Recommended Readings:

Kapchits, G.L. (1980). “Syntax of the Somali Language.” Moscow University Press.
Kapchits, G.L. (2005). “Somali Proverbs: Collection and Commentary.”
Lewis, I.M. (2000). “A Grammatical Sketch of Somali.”
